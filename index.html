About URJA 3.0

URJA 3.0 is a flagship annual sports festival hosted by the Department of Physical Education and Sports of B.K. Birla College, Kalyan. The fest brings together students to participate in a variety of competitive and recreational sports events, promoting physical fitness, discipline, leadership, and unity.
